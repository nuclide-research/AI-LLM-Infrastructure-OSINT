FP CATALOG
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. RAGFlow returns HTTP 200 with inner `code` field for all responses.
   Do NOT use HTTP status as auth signal; match against inner code.
   (Insight: distinguish identity-confirmed-by-fingerprint vs
   auth-state-by-data-layer.)

2. The /v1/system/config endpoint is intentionally public — it does
   not constitute a "vulnerability" by itself. The exposure of
   registerEnabled=1 IS the finding. Severity = HIGH for individual
   hosts, MEDIUM at population scale (informs targeting).

3. Several Shenzhen Middle School-class findings exist in the 618.
   These require careful handling: K-12 / minor data context elevates
   risk classification, but jurisdiction and language considerations
   complicate direct outreach. Default to vendor-mediated disclosure.

RESEARCH CONTRIBUTION
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This survey adds the highest auth-permissive default rate measured in
NuClide's 2026 survey program (88.9% Langfuse vs 68.7% Flowise, 11.8%
Open WebUI, 0.9% Dify). It strengthens Candidate Insight #76 (auth-on-
default rate is platform-cohort dependent) and supports the actionable
hypothesis that public population surveys with upstream disclosure
measurably move the rate within 2-3 minor-version cycles.

================================================================================
NEW PROMETHEUS-SPECIFIC FINDING — INSIGHT #91 CANDIDATE
================================================================================

/api/v1/status/config dumps the FULL prometheus.yml configuration on 100%
of open hosts. The endpoint returns:

  - scrape_configs[*].static_configs[*].targets (overlaps with /targets but
    more authoritative)
  - remote_write[*].url (where metrics forward; pivot targets)
  - alerting.alertmanagers[*].static_configs[*].targets (AlertManager URLs
    on internal networks)
  - basic_auth.password / bearer_token (when present in inline static
    configs, EMBEDDED IN PLAINTEXT in the response)

25 of 475 hosts (5.3%) have embedded credentials in their leaked config.

Hosts with embedded creds — distribution (no IPs):
  by country: FR 5, DE 4, SG 4, US 3, FI 2, TW 1, IE 1, IN 1, AT 1, CN 1,
              CA 1, VN 1, ES 1, KR 1, UK 1
  by org: Hetzner 5, DigitalOcean 4, Google 3, OVH 2, Scaleway 2,
          (long tail of 1-host orgs)

INSIGHT #91 CANDIDATE: any platform with a "give me everything about your
configuration" debug endpoint open by default produces a richer disclosure
than the platform's own data-plane endpoints. Same structural class as
Consul /v1/agent/self, Vault /v1/sys/config/state/sanitized, NATS /varz.

RESTRAINT DISCIPLINE (what we did NOT do)

  - Zero write-method HTTP calls on any endpoint.
  - No /api/v1/query?query=* reads of metric VALUES — schema only.

    public artifact. The credentials are not used.

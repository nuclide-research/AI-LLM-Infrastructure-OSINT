## Research-program relevance

Defender-side role responsible for IDS/IPS, firewall ACLs, VPN, antivirus, and RMF documentation around dedicated cyber defense systems. In the NuClide AI/LLM infrastructure research program, this is the operator-mirror role on the target side: when assessing how an organization detects and contains exposed inference endpoints or vector DB attack surface, this is the person tuning the sensors and writing the access controls we map against. Useful for disclosure routing (they own the IDS/firewall change) and for threat-modeling defender response timelines against AI infra scans.

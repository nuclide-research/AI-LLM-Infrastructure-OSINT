## Research-program relevance

Directly relevant as the disclosure-recipient role. When NuClide surfaces unauthenticated AI/ML infrastructure, the ISSM is the named accountability function on the receiving organization's side — the human who owns the POA&M (T0264) and the audit-finding tracking (T0234) for the vuln we just disclosed. Understanding ISSM tasks (T0089 evaluation/validation/implementation of security improvements, T0157 awareness program) shapes how to write disclosure reports that map cleanly into the recipient's existing workflow: POA&M-ready, RMF-aware, and tied to specific SA&A deliverables.

## Research-program relevance

Directly relevant as a disclosure-recipient role and as an internal-discipline analog. The role exists precisely because the data-class problem NuClide solves at scale (PII/PHI/PCI exposure via unauth AI/ML infrastructure) is what Privacy Officers own organizationally. Tasks T0032 (PIA), T0903/T0904 (proposed-rules PIA, periodic compliance monitoring), and T0916 (breach management) define their inbound work — exactly what an aimap unauth-Weaviate-PII finding triggers. KSA K0066 (PIAs) and competency C014 inform our schema-only data-sensitivity classification discipline (bag-of-fields, not exfil). Also paired with 731-Cyber Legal Advisor (14% pairing) — disclosure reports for PII findings should route to this dyad.

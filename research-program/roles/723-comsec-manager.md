## Research-program relevance

Tangentially relevant. COMSEC focuses on key custody and crypto material — not the AI/LLM infrastructure layer we assess. Exception: when an aimap finding surfaces exposed crypto material (private keys, JWT signing secrets, KMS endpoints), the receiving COMSEC manager (key custodian role) is the named accountability function for revocation and re-keying. Encryption (C017) knowledge informs how we classify exposed material in disclosure reports.

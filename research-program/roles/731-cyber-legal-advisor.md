## Research-program relevance

Directly relevant to disclosure-recipient routing. The Cyber Legal Advisor is invoked when NuClide findings touch foreign-disclosure regimes (K0341 import/export, ITAR), insider-threat investigations (K0107), or constitutional/legal-process boundaries on ICS/critical-infrastructure findings (K0267). When aimap-profile flags a target as defense-contractor or controlled-data, the disclosure routing chain often terminates at this role on the recipient side. Also relevant to NuClide's own scope/authorization posture: legal grounds for active probing and the CISA disclosure ethic.

## Research-program relevance

Directly relevant. 461 is the disclosure-recipient role: when NuClide issues a finding, the org-side analyst who triages it sits here. Tasks T0469/T0470 (security posture trends), T0485 (resolve vulnerabilities), T0526 (cybersecurity recommendations to leadership) are exactly the workflow a NuClide disclosure feeds. KSA K0339 (network analysis tools to identify vulnerabilities), S0036/S0141/S0167 (evaluate/assess/recognize) define the analyst's side of the conversation. The clean way to frame a NuClide finding is to give the 461 everything they need to validate (verified read, fingerprint signal, repro probe) and bracket the surface so they can scope mitigation against T0485.

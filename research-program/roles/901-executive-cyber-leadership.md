## Research-program relevance

The disclosure-recipient endpoint for high-severity NuClide findings. CISO/CSO/SAISO is the named accountability function when a finding warrants C-suite escalation — exposed PHI/PII at scale, defense-contractor data leakage, critical-infrastructure exposure (T0282). Tasks T0148 (CND guidance publishing for the enterprise constituency) and T0130 (external interface for incident dissemination) are the recipient-side workflows our disclosure reports feed into. A0129 (integrate info security mgmt with strategic/operational planning) and S0357 (anticipate new security threats) describe the strategic context the Executive needs to act on a NuClide finding — the framing our reporting should match: business risk in dollars, narrative arc from anonymous to impact, remediation that is fixable in the real world.

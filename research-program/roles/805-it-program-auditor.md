## Research-program relevance

Directly relevant as a process-discipline analog. S0085 (skill in conducting audits or reviews of technical systems) is the formal-audit equivalent of NuClide's verification-stage methodology. Both produce evidence-based, repeatable findings against a published standard. T0072 (methods to monitor and measure risk, compliance, assurance) maps onto VisorLog's findings-ledger discipline and VisorScuba's compliance scoring. When a NuClide disclosure arrives at an organization, the IT Program Auditor is one of the recipient roles likely to validate, score, and track remediation through audit findings.

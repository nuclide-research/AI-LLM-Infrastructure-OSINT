     sockets, futex hang). Root cause: 6s timeout is connect-only, no per-read

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 5.  CANDIDATE INSIGHTS (codify-every-survey)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   I-A  Version-spread legitimacy discriminator: a uniform version across a whole
        banner cohort (all Milvus/2.3.4) is a deception tell; organic populations
        show spread (weaviate 1.28-1.36). Pair with a body-coherence probe
        (/metrics families) to confirm header-spoof.
   I-B  Metrics-body coherence > banner: a host can spoof a Server header and a
        200 /healthz but cannot synthesize a coherent /metrics families body.
        The metrics endpoint is the cheap real-vs-spoof discriminator for Milvus.
   I-C  Snapshot listing = the Cat-13 exfil primitive: on an unauth vector DB the
        backup endpoint (qdrant /snapshots, weaviate /v1/backups, ES /_snapshot)
        turns "open surface" into "full-DB download" with one GET. The name IS
        the finding; the listing proves the primitive without exercising it.
   I-D  Scanner-injected collections (probe/rce/cve-test) are a contamination
        layer that doubles as proof of unauth-WRITE; strip from data-inventory
        findings but log as a write-exposure signal.

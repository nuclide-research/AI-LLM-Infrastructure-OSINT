  CLAIM 1 (WRONG): Tabby is Shodan-dark (population estimate 150-600 live).
  REALITY: Tabby is Shodan-visible at 94 hosts via http.title:"Tabby".
           Squad-1's port:8080-constrained dorks missed 89/94 = 94.7%.
  ROOT CAUSE: squad fixated on --port flag default (8080) instead of
              docker-compose quickstart default (9090).
  CODIFIED: Insight #93.

  CLAIM 2 (WRONG): GET /v1/health is always unauthenticated on all Tabby versions.
  REALITY: 5.78.203.59:8080 (ide.cohesia.ai, confirmed Tabby v0.11.0+ webserver)
           returns 401 Unauthorized on /v1/health, /v1beta/models, and
           /v1beta/server_setting. The "always-open" claim was extracted from
           upstream documentation but the actual webserver-mode implementation
           gates everything except the SPA shell when JWT/admin is configured.
  CODIFIED: Insight #94 (hybrid Tier-A*/C platforms).

  CLAIM 3 (WRONG): No nuclei templates exist for TabbyML.
  REALITY: s4e-io/tabby-panel.yaml exists in projectdiscovery/nuclei-templates,
           matchers on /auth/signin → status 200 + <title>Tabby - Sign In.

  CLAIM 4 (WRONG, squad-3): No aimap fingerprint exists for Sourcegraph.
  REALITY: Sourcegraph FP exists at ai-recon/aimap/fingerprints.go:2634,
           anchored on body_contains "Private mode requires authentication"
           (locked-cohort signal) + "Sign in - Sourcegraph" (title signal).

VERDICT
  CONFIRMED HONEYPOT FLEET (operator-grade, multi-layer staged deception).

  Evidence
    - 66/66 protocol-correct MCP servers (identical backend, identical toolset)
    - I/N = 0.833 cert-issuer-distribution-overload (Insight #97)
    - L7 multi-service emulation across disjoint verticals (Insight #32)
    - 100% AWS EC2 multi-region spread (single deployment target)
    - Tool-name surface is bait: get_aws_admin_credentials, schedule_commands,
      add_cron_job - designed to attract LLM-agent reconnaissance traffic

  Operator hypothesis (not yet attributed)
    Likely defender / research telemetry honeypot (Microsoft Defender, AWS
    GuardDuty internal canaries, Shadowserver, GreyNoise, or similar).
    Sophistication exceeds AS63949 single-issuer fleet.

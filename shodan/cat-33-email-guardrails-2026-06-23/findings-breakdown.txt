## CATEGORY INSIGHT CANDIDATE

Cat-33's thin Shodan surface is structural. All major vendors bind to loopback or
deploy as pure SaaS APIs. The discoverable surface is misconfigured dev deployments
where the operator forgot to bind to localhost or add auth.

Auth-on-default thesis: CONFIRMED for Cat-33 production deployments.
The one unauth instance is a dev/staging mistake (v0.1.0, 3 days old, ephemeral Azure IP).

Key secondary insight: the bypass recipe problem. When a guardrail's policy is
public (via unauth read), the guardrail is useless -- an attacker has the exact
pattern they must avoid. Defender intent vs attacker knowledge asymmetry
collapses entirely. Security controls require confidentiality of the control definition.

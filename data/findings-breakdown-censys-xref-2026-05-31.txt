CANDIDATE INSIGHT #70 -- Censys is a DUAL primitive, not one
------------------------------------------------------------
Censys cross-reference yields two separable things:
  (a) full-range port coverage -> surfaces data-tier / second-app shadow a curated AI-port
      sweep cannot see (Perplexica 1->10, LightRAG 1->7).
  (b) protocol auth-decoders   -> postgres.startup_error and redis.auth_response classify
      the shadow's auth-state passively, downgrading "exposed DB" to "auth enforced" with
      ZERO host connections.
Discipline: a Censys port + DATABASE label is IDENTITY (surface open). The decoder is
AUTH-STATE. Never conflate them. This extends Insight #16 (a 200 is identity not auth) to
the data tier, and pairs with Insight #69 (a Shodan-dark port is not a host negative).

THESIS CONTRIBUTION
  Kubeflow at 0.8% population unauth is the cleanest BREAK in the
  auth-permissive-cohort thesis to date. Where Langfuse / RAGFlow /
  ComfyUI / Ray all sit at 9-89% unauth, Kubeflow at 0.8% says:
  default-auth that requires non-trivial deploy work (Dex + Istio +
  oidc-authservice) is sufficient to flip a platform out of the
  cohort. The 5 unauth are operators who deliberately deployed
  single-user mode (apiServerMultiUser=false) — operator-side
  decision, not platform-default exposure.

  This strengthens Insight #76 by giving it a clean control:
    auth-permissive-by-default               -> 9-89% unauth at scale
    auth-required-by-default (even if complex) -> sub-1% unauth at scale
  The deploy-friction story still applies: Dex+Istio setup is the
  cost operators pay to get auth-on.
